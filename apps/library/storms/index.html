SEVERE-WEATHER RESOLVER

Lightning animation with environmental response

Storm motion needs more than a repeating bolt. Atmoscene coordinates irregular, branching strikes with short cloud, rain and full-sky illumination. Wind and precipitation remain separate inputs so intensity can change without replaying one generic loop.

Tropical thunderstorms and cyclones extend the system with rotating cloud bands, marine surfaces and stronger directional flow. Blizzards combine storm-level wind with snow density, drift and whiteout layers instead of borrowing a rain animation.

InputSeverity + precipitation

Storm code, rain or snow intensity, gust ratio and local sky phase drive the scene.

ComposeCloud + strike + flash

Lightning branches vary while the full scene responds with brief illumination.

OutputAccessible severe scene

Text-safe zones remain protected and reduced motion removes rapid flashing.

Multiple strike patterns

Branching, timing and intensity vary so lightning does not feel robotic or repeat on an obvious beat.

Full-scene illumination

Cloud field, precipitation and sky brighten briefly with every major strike.

Composable severity

Rain, snow, wind, cloud density and lightning can scale independently from one another.
