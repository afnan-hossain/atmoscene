WINTER-WEATHER RESOLVER

Different winter conditions need different motion

Flurries remain sparse and slow. Light and moderate snow increase particle density gradually. Heavy snow adds accumulation, blowing snow follows the wind vector, and blizzards combine fast flow, drift and controlled whiteout.

Freezing fog uses layered low visibility with ice-crystal detail; diamond dust uses sparse suspended shimmer under a clearer sky. White artwork receives edge contrast and shadow treatment so it remains readable over both pale daylight and dark night gradients.
