MOTION RESOLVER

Distinct flow for different wind conditions

A breeze should not look like a slower squall. Atmoscene changes stream length, curvature, spacing, speed, cloud displacement and environmental particles while keeping movement directional and fluid.

Windsock, compass and Beaufort graphics belong in weather-data cards, not inside the sky. The scene resolver uses speed, gust ratio, direction and condition to choose the appropriate motion profile without rotating unrelated artwork.

InputSpeed + gusts + bearing

Wind speed, peak gust, direction and weather condition establish intensity and flow.

ComposeProfile + environment

Streams become cloud flow, rain shear, laminar fog, snow drift or turbulent motion.

OutputDirectional SVG motion

Fluid paths reverse and tilt correctly while text-safe areas remain protected.

Calm to open-air flow

Sparse, longer streams build from light breeze to continuous directional movement.

Condition-aware motion

Clouds translate, rain shears, fog flows in layers, snow drifts and aerosols follow the same bearing.

Gusting and turbulent

Short faster packets and denser flow communicate instability without making the scene bounce.
